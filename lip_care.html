Lip Care

To have smooth lips you have to put a lot of effort. But with our tips and tricks of lip care, you most certainly going to have healthy and soft lips.

Don't forget your lip balm

Lip balm is the key for smooth lips. Make sure not to forget to put it in your purse or pocket. If you apply it regulary your lips will feel soft trough out the day. As well as their nourishing power, lip balms also act as a protective layer, blocking the damaging effects of outside elements.

Protect your lips from the sun

Use a lip balm with sun protection like Labello Sun Protect to keep your lips protected and moisturised while you’re out enjoying the sun. Lips can be heavily affected by the sun, because the skin on them is thin and delicate. Remember to keep reapplying sun protection to ensure your lips are always covered.

You don't always have to use heavy lipstick

Certain Labello lip balm products like Soft Rosé Lip Balm provides smooth lips with a subtle touch of colour as they nourish. This is perfect when you want to keep your lips healty and soft, but have to go out to have a dinner with your date.

Return moisture back to your lips

Lip butters provide a different texture than lip balms do, they leave your lips looking shiny, plump and hydrated. Labello Lip Butters contains natural oils, which provide your lips with intense moisture and long-lasting care.
