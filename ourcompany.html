Our Company

As one of the leading international skin care companies we are close to consumers, offering them compelling, innovative products.
Our brands are trusted universally – from NIVEA, one of the world's largest skin care brands, to other internationally successful brands such as Eucerin, La Prairie, Labello, 8×4, and Hansaplast/Elastoplast.
With more than 130 years of experience in skin care and one of the world's most modern skin research centers, Beiersdorf stands for innovative and high-quality cosmetic products.
